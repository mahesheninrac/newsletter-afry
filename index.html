About Us

Eninrac Consulting Pvt Ltd

We are a dedicated team of over 400 energy energy specialists, advising clients across all areas of the energy transition. From thermal generation to offshore wind, trading to sustainability, corporate strategy to organisational and digital transformation, our mission is to accelerate the transition towards a sustainable society.

Our Services

Market Analysis

Our market analysis arm helps clients understand and model energy markets and their impact from a commercial, regulatory and technological perspective.

Strategy

We consult on all areas of corporate and business strategy, including market entry and expansion, resilience, portfolio-level and asset-level decarbonisation.

Transaction Advisory

Our Transactions line support clients throughout the entire transaction process, including pre-transaction support, wide- ranging due diligence capabilities and post- transaction integration.
